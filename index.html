Pollute AI: Detecting pollution one pixel at a time

Description

PollutAI is an advanced image classification project that leverages cutting-edge machine learning techniques to assess air quality based on images. The project is trained on a dataset based on the India-Nepal Air Pollution Dataset , which contains a diverse collection of images from various locations in India and Nepal, capturing the air pollution levels in those regions.

In our Model, we have 6 different classes: Severe, Very unhealthy, Unhealthy, Unhealthy for sensitive groups, Moderate, and Good.
